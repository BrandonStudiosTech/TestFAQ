Platform Quick Start Apple Migration Steps

Platform Paths

ANDROID / ROKU / AMAZON

Usually just update and sign in

  1. Update the BTV app from your device app store/channel store.
  2. Open the app and sign in with your existing email.
  3. If sign-in fails, reset your password and try again.
  4. After reset, use the same credentials on all devices.

APPLE (IPHONE / IPAD / APPLE TV)

Old app users may need restore + reactivation

  1. Delete the old app version.
  2. Install the newest app.
  3. Tap Restore Purchase before subscribing again.
  4. Create or reset password when prompted.

APPLE SUPPORT

Apple App Migration and Reactivation

  1. Delete the old app

    On iPhone, press and hold the old BTV app icon, tap Remove App, then confirm Delete App.

  2. Install the latest BTV app

    Download the newest app from the App Store, then make sure your device is signed in with the same Apple ID used for your original subscription.

  3. Tap Restore Purchase first

    Open the new app and do not tap Subscribe yet. Scroll the home screen and tap Restore Purchase.

  4. Create or reset your account password

    After restore succeeds, create your password (or reset it) when prompted. This activates your account for app and web login.

  5. Sign in everywhere

    Use that same email and new password to sign in on mobile, tablet, TV, and web.

Important

If Restore Purchase does not work, first confirm you are signed into the exact Apple ID that purchased the original subscription.
